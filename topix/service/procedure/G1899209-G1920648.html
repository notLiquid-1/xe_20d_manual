Removal
CAUTION:

The lower arm ball joint can be damaged by excessive articulation. The wheel knuckle must be fully supported at all times. Do not allow the wheel knuckle to hang on the lower arm. Failure to follow this instruction will result in damage to vehicle.

NOTES:

  • Some components shown removed for clarity.

  • Removal steps in this procedure may contain installation details.

  • Some variation in the illustrations may occur, but the essential information is always correct.
