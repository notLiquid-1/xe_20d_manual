Rear End Sheet Metal Repairs

Spare Wheel Well Reinforcement (G1797581)

REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION

Loading...

Removal
NOTE:

In this procedure the spare wheel well reinforcement is installed in conjunction with:

  • Rear bumper
  • Rear armature
  • Rear bumper mounting
  • Back panel outer
  • Back panel inner
  • Exhaust system
  • Fuel tank
  • Rear subframe
  • Spare wheel well
  1. The spare wheel well reinforcement is serviced as indicated.
  2. Before commencing this procedure make sure that you are aware of all health and safety requirements. For additional information, refer to: Health and Safety Precautions (100-00 General Information, Description and Operation).
  3. For further information on methods, tools and fixings used in this procedure refer to the body repairs - general information section.
    4. NOTES:

    • Any joints sealed as part of vehicle manufacture must be sealed as part of the repair.

    • Make sure a zinc rich primer is applied to any bare metal surfaces.

  4. For additional information, refer to: Rear Bumper (501-19, Removal and Installation).
  5. For additional information, refer to: Rear Armature (501-19, Removal and Installation).
  6. For additional information, refer to: Rear Bumper Mounting (501-30 Rear End Sheet Metal Repairs, Removal and Installation).
  7. For additional information, refer to: Back Panel Outer (501-30 Rear End Sheet Metal Repairs, Removal and Installation).
  8. For additional information, refer to: Back Panel Inner (501-30 Rear End Sheet Metal Repairs, Removal and Installation).
  9. For additional information, refer to: Exhaust System (309-00A, Removal and Installation) / Exhaust System - Vehicles With: Diesel Exhaust Fluid (309-00B, Removal and Installation) / Exhaust System (309-00C, Removal and Installation) / Exhaust System - Vehicles Without: Diesel Exhaust Fluid (309-00B, Removal and Installation).
  10. For additional information, refer to: Fuel Tank (310-01 Fuel Tank and Lines - V6 S/C 3.0L Petrol , Petrol, Removal and Installation).
  11. For additional information, refer to: Rear Subframe (502-00, Removal and Installation).
  12. For additional information, refer to: Spare Wheel Well (501-30 Rear End Sheet Metal Repairs, Removal and Installation).
  13. Remove the spot welds as indicated.
  14. Carefully separate the joints and remove the spare wheel well reinforcement.
Installation
  1. Dress the flanges where necessary.
  2. Offer up the new spare wheel well reinforcement and clamp into position. Check alignment if correct proceed to next step, if not rectify and recheck before proceeding.
  3. Remove the spare wheel well reinforcement.
  4. Clean and prepare the panel surfaces.
  5. Offer up the new spare wheel well reinforcement and clamp into position. Check alignment if correct proceed to next step, if not rectify and recheck before proceeding.
  6. Install spot welds as indicated.
  7. Make sure that any open or exposed panel joints are correctly sealed.
  8. Make sure corrosion protection is applied to all areas affected by repair.
  9. The installation of associated panels and components is the reversal of removal procedure.
