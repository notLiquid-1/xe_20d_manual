Removal
NOTE:

In this procedure the side panel is installed in conjunction with:

  • Front bumper
  • Fender
  • Fender splash shield
  • Windshield
  • Rear window
  • Front door
  • Rear door
  • Rocker panel moulding
  • Headliner
  • Side air curtain module
  • Roof panel
  • Rear bumper
  • Rear lamp assembly
  • Front seat
  • Rear seat cushion
  • Instrument panel
  • Luggage compartment lid
  • Fuel tank filler pipe
  1. The side panel is serviced as indicated.
  2. Before commencing this procedure make sure that you are aware of all Health and Safety requirements.For additional information, refer to: Health and Safety Precautions (100-00 General Information, Description and Operation).
  3. For further information on the methods, tools and fixings used in this procedure refer to the body repairs - general information section.
    4. NOTES:

    • Where breakstem fasteners (BSF) removal is instructed remove fastener shaft with a 4mm punch and remove the head with a 6.5mm drill bit. Clean all the drilled holes to make sure they are free from debris.

    • Where self piercing rivets (SPR's) removal is instructed use either the approved SPR tool or 5.3mm drill bit.

    • Any joints sealed as part of vehicle manufacture must be sealed as part of the repair.

  4. For additional information, refer to: Front Bumper (501-19 Bumpers, Removal and Installation).
  5. For additional information, refer to: Fender (501-02 Front End Body Panels, Removal and Installation).
  6. For additional information, refer to: Fender Splash Shield (501-02 Front End Body Panels, Removal and Installation).
  7. For additional information, refer to: Windshield (501-11 Glass, Frames and Mechanisms, Removal and Installation).
  8. For additional information, refer to: Rear Window (501-11 Glass, Frames and Mechanisms, Removal and Installation).
  9. For additional information, refer to: Front Door (501-03 Body Closures, Removal and Installation).
  10. For additional information, refer to: Rear Door (501-03 Body Closures, Removal and Installation).
  11. For additional information, refer to: Rocker Panel Moulding (501-08 Exterior Trim and Ornamentation, Removal and Installation).
  12. For additional information, refer to: Headliner (501-05 Interior Trim and Ornamentation, Removal and Installation).
  13. For additional information, refer to: Side Air Curtain Module (501-20B Supplementary Restraint System, Removal and Installation).
  14. For additional information, refer to: Roof Panel - Vehicles With: Roof Opening Panel (501-28 Roof Sheet Metal Repairs, Removal and Installation) / Roof Panel - Vehicles Without: Roof Opening Panel (501-28 Roof Sheet Metal Repairs, Removal and Installation).
  15. For additional information, refer to: Rear Bumper (501-19 Bumpers, Removal and Installation).
  16. For additional information, refer to: Rear Lamp Assembly (417-01 Exterior Lighting, Removal and Installation).
  17. For additional information, refer to: Front Row Seat (501-10 Seating, Removal and Installation).
  18. For additional information, refer to: Rear Seat Cushion (501-10 Seating, Removal and Installation).
  19. For additional information, refer to: Instrument Panel Console (501-12 Instrument Panel and Console, Removal and Installation) / Instrument Panel Lower Trim Panel (501-12 Instrument Panel and Console, Removal and Installation).
  20. For additional information, refer to: Luggage Compartment Lid (501-03 Body Closures, Removal and Installation).
  21. NOTE:

    Right hand only.

    For additional information, refer to: Fuel Tank Filler Pipe (310-01C Fuel Tank and Lines - V6 S/C 3.0L Petrol , Petrol, Removal and Installation).
  22. Release and position the floor carpet to one side.
  23. Release and position the floor noise, vibration and harshness (NVH) material to one side.
  24. Release and position the wiring harness to one side.
  25. Remove the SPR's as indicated.
  26. Remove the SPR's as indicated.
  27. Remove the SPR's as indicated.
  28. Remove the SPR's as indicated.
  29. Remove the SPR's as indicated.
  30. Remove the SPR's as indicated.
  31. Remove the SPR's as indicated.
  32. Remove the SPR's as indicated.
  33. Build an expansion space between the quarter panel and the water drain panel as indicated.
  34. Carefully separate the side panel joints avoiding damage to the NVH components as indicated.
  35. CAUTION:

    This step requires the aid of another technician as the side panel is heavy.

    Remove the side panel.
Installation
CAUTION:

Where structural adhesive is to be applied between surfaces it is essential compression is applied to the joints during the curing period.

NOTES:

  • BSF installation involves using the Jaguar approved BSF installation tool and requires a hole diameter of 6.5mm.

  • SPR installation requires using the Jaguar approved SPR tool.

  1. Remove the rivet remnants.
  2. Dress the flanges where necessary.
  3. CAUTION:

    This step requires the aid of another technician as the side panel is heavy.

    Offer up the new side panel and clamp into position. Check alignment if correct proceed to next step, if not rectify and recheck before proceeding.
  4. Drill holes where the BSF's are to be installed as indicated.
  5. Drill holes where the BSF's and the MIG plug welds are to be installed as indicated.
  6. Drill holes where the BSF's are to be installed as indicated.
  7. Drill holes where the BSF's are to be installed as indicated.
  8. Drill holes where the BSF's are to be installed as indicated.
  9. Offer up the roof panel and clamp into position. Check alignment if correct proceed to next step, if not rectify and recheck before proceeding.
  10. Measure and mark the positions where holes for the BSF's are to be drilled as indicated.
  11. Measure and mark the positions where holes for the BSF's are to be drilled as indicated.
  12. Drill holes where the BSF's are to be installed as indicated.
  13. Drill holes where the BSF's are to be installed as indicated.
  14. Remove the roof panel.
  15. CAUTION:

    This step requires the aid of another technician as the side panel is heavy.

    Remove the new side panel.
  16. Deburr the drilled holes.
  17. Using a fine bristle disc clean and prepare the panel surfaces including the NVH components.
  18. NOTES:

    • Make sure that the SPR's are installed before the installation of the side panel.

    • When the side panel is installed, make sure that the area around the SPR's is clamped until the bonding material has cured.

    Install the SPR's as indicated.
  19. NOTES:

    • Make sure that the SPR's are installed before the installation of the side panel.

    • When the side panel is installed, make sure that the area around the SPR's is clamped until the bonding material has cured.

    Install the SPR's as indicated.
  20. Apply the coupling agent where the Jaguar recommended bonding material is to be applied and allow to dry.
  21. NOTES:

    • Make sure a continuous bead of adhesive surrounds fixing holes.

    • Do not apply adhesive in the vicinity of the MIG plug welds.

    Apply a 5mm zig zag bead of the Jaguar recommended bonding material as indicated.
  22. CAUTION:

    This step requires the aid of another technician as the side panel is heavy.

    Offer up the new side panel and clamp into position. Check alignment if correct proceed to next step, if not rectify and recheck before proceeding.
  23. Make sure that the side panel is clamped in position until the structural adhesive has fully cured as indicated.
  24. Make sure that the side panel is clamped in position until the structural adhesive has fully cured as indicated.
  25. Install the BSF's and the MIG plug welds as indicated.
  26. Install the SPR's and the BSF's as indicated.
  27. Install the SPR's as indicated.
  28. Install the SPR's as indicated.
  29. Install the SPR's as indicated.
  30. Install the SPR's and the BSF's as indicated.
  31. Install the BSF's as indicated.
  32. Install the SPR and the BSF's as indicated.
  33. NOTE:

    Make sure that these BSF's are installed once the roof panel has been installed.

    Install the BSF's as indicated.
  34. NOTE:

    Make sure that these BSF's are installed once the roof panel has been installed.

    Install the BSF's as indicated.
  35. Dress the MIG plug welds.
  36. Remove any excess adhesive.
  37. Install the front door upper and lower door hinges and tighten the bolts to 30Nm.
  38. Install the rear door upper and lower door hinges and tighten the bolts to 30Nm.
  39. Make sure that any open or exposed panel joints are correctly sealed.
  40. Make sure that all installed SPR's are sealed using a Jaguar recommended sealer.
  41. Make sure corrosion protection is applied to all areas affected by repair.
  42. The installation of associated panels and components is the reversal of removal procedure.
