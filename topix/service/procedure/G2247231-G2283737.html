Draining
NOTE:
  • This procedure contains some variation in the illustrations depending on the vehicle specification, but the essential information is always correct.
  • This procedure contains illustrations showing certain components removed to provide extra clarity.

  1. Raise and support the vehicle on a suitable 2 post lift.
    Refer to: Lifting (100-02 Jacking and Lifting, Description and Operation).
  2. Remove the air deflector.
    Refer to: Engine Undershield (501-02 Front End Body Panels, Removal and Installation).
    1. Remove the oil filler plug, do not discard.
      Special Tool(s): 307-452
    • NOTE:

      Use a suitable container to collect escaping fluid.

      Remove and discard the transmission drain plug.
    • Allow the transmission fluid to drain into the container.
    • Install a new transmission drain plug.
      Renew Part: Transmission fluid drain plug Quantity: 1.
      Torque: 8 Nm
    • Clean the area around the fluid filler plug, wipe away any excess fluid.
Filling
  1. Fill the transmission with the recommended transmission oil until fluid flows out of the oil filler hole.
    Refer to: Specifications (307-01 Automatic Transmission/Transaxle - Vehicles With: 8HP45 8-Speed Automatic Transmission RWD, Specifications).
  2. Allow the transmission fluid to drain from the oil filler hole until the flow stops.
  3. Using the special tool, install the original oil filler plug.
    Special Tool(s): 307-452
  4. Remove the special tool.
    Special Tool(s): 307-452
  5. Clean the area around the filler plug, wipe away any excess fluid and remove the container.
  6. Lower the vehicle.
  7. NOTE:

    Make sure that the transmission is not in the default mode, if this is the case, stop and correct the cause first and then restart the fluid level check.

    1. Connect the Jaguar Land Rover approved diagnostic equipment to the vehicle.
      General Equipment: Jaguar Land Rover diagnostic equipment
    1. Start the engine and allow to idle.
    1. Switch off the air conditioning (A/C) system and other electrical components.
    • Apply and hold the brake pedal.
    • Select reverse gear and wait for 10 seconds.
    • Select drive, then manual 1st gear and wait for 10 seconds.
    • Select 2nd gear and wait for 10 seconds.
    • Select Park (P) on the TCS.
  9. Raise the engine speed to 2000 rpm and hold for 30 seconds. Then return to idle speed.
    • Using the Jaguar Land Rover approved diagnostic equipment, check the transmission fluid temperature.
    • If the transmission oil temperature is lower than 30°C (86°F). Allow the transmission to warm up above 30°C (86°F) with the engine idling in the (P) position, then proceed with the transmission fluid level check.
    • If the transmission oil temperature is above 50°C (122°F), switch off the engine and allow the transmission to cool down, then repeat steps 13 and 14.
    • Remove and discard the oil filler plug.
      Special Tool(s): 307-452
    • If fluid flows out of the oil filler hole, proceed to step 18.
    • If fluid does not flow out of the oil filler hole, proceed to step 17.
  12. Fill the transmission with the recommended transmission oil until fluid flows out of the oil filler hole.
    Refer to: Specifications (307-01 Automatic Transmission/Transaxle - Vehicles With: 8HP45 8-Speed Automatic Transmission RWD, Specifications).
  13. Allow the transmission fluid to drain from the oil filler hole until the flow stops.
  14. Using the special tool, install a new oil filler plug.
    Special Tool(s): 307-452
    Renew Part: Transmission fill plug Quantity: 1.
  15. CAUTION:

    Make sure the transmission fluid fill plug is tightened to the correct specification. Failure to follow this instruction may result in damage to the transmission.

    • To make sure the transmission fill plug is torqued to the correct specification. Using the special tool and torque wrench the following calculation steps must be followed.
    • Step 1. Multiply 35 Nm by the effective length of the torque wrench (1).
    • Step 2. Add the effective length of the special tool (2) to the effective length of the torque wrench (1).
    • Step 3. Divide the total of step 1 by the total of step 2.
    • Step 4. Set the torque wrench to the figure arrived at in step 3.
    • Tighten the transmission fluid fill plug to the torque given by the calculation.
  16. Clean the area around the filler plug, wipe away any excess fluid and remove the container.
  17. Install the air deflector.
    Refer to: Engine Undershield (501-02 Front End Body Panels, Removal and Installation).
  18. Lower the vehicle.
  19. Disconnect the Jaguar Land Rover approved diagnostic equipment from the vehicle.
