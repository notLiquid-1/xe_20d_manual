Removal
NOTES:

  • This procedure contains some variation in the illustrations depending on the vehicle specification, but the essential information is always correct.

  • This procedure contains illustrations showing certain components removed to provide extra clarity.


  1. Disconnect the startup battery ground cable.
    Refer to: Startup Battery Disconnect and Connect (414-01 Battery, Mounting and Cables, General Procedures).
  2. Make the airbag supplementary restraint system (SRS) safe.
    Refer to: Standard Workshop Practices (100-00 General Information, Description and Operation).
  3. NOTE:

    Repeat the step for the other side.

    Remove the side air curtain module.
    Refer to: Side Air Curtain Module (501-20B Supplementary Restraint System, Removal and Installation).
  4. Disconnect the roof opening panel electrical connector.
  5. NOTE:

    Repeat the step for the other side.

    Remove the A-piller outer finisher.
  6. NOTE:

    Repeat this step for the other side.

    Remove the roof mouldings.
  7. Using a suitable adhesive tape, protect the surrounding paintwork.
    General Equipment: Adhesive tape
  8. CAUTION:

    Make sure not to damage the alignment pins when cutting the bonding material.

    Use a glazing cutting wire to cut the adhesive.
    General Equipment: Windscreen removal / installation equipment
  9. NOTE:

    This step requires the aid of another technician.

    Remove the roof opening panel frame assembly.
  10. Remove and discard the 6 roof opening panel spacers.
Installation
  1. CAUTION:

    Make sure that the mating faces are clean and free of foreign material.

    Remove all residual adhesives from the mating faces.
  2. CAUTION:

    Make sure you are using new roof opening panel spacers positioned on the correct locations.

    NOTE:

    This step only applies if the original component is installed.

    Install the 6 new roof opening panel spacers.
    Renew Part: Roof opening panel frame fitting kit Quantity: 1.
  3. Apply an 8 mm wide, 12 mm high bead of adhesive to the position illustrated.
    Renew Part: Glass bonding adhesive kit Quantity: 1.
  4. NOTE:

    This step requires the aid of another technician.

    Make sure the alignment pins are located in the correct position when installing the frame assembly to the vehicle.
    • Install the roof opening panel frame assembly.
    • Test the sealer for leaks, apply additional sealer if necessary. If water is used, allow sealer to dry before testing. Spray water around the glass and check for leaks. Mark any area that leaks. Dry the glass and sealer then apply additional sealer.
  6. CAUTIONS:

    • The vehicle must remain stationary during the adhesive curing period.

    • The vehicle must remain in the workshop for at least 8 hours after the adhesive is applied.

    Check the gap around is even and the roof opening panel frame is flush with the roof panel as illustrated.
  7. Install the roof mouldings.
  8. Install the A-piller outer finishers.
  9. Connect the roof opening panel electrical connector.
  10. NOTE:

    Repeat the step for the other side.

    Install the side air curtain module.
    Refer to: Side Air Curtain Module (501-20B Supplementary Restraint System, Removal and Installation).
  11. Connect the startup battery ground cable.
    Refer to: Startup Battery Disconnect and Connect (414-01 Battery, Mounting and Cables, General Procedures).
