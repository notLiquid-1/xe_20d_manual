Fender Apron Panel (G1784978)

REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION

Loading...

Removal
NOTE:

In this procedure the fender apron panel is installed in conjunction with:

  • Front bumper
  • Front fender
  • Fender splash shield
  • Hood
  • Pedestrian protection hood actuator
  • Hood latch panel
  • Anti-lock braking system (ABS) module
  • Windshield washer reservoir
  • Air cleaner
  • Coolant expansion tank
  • Charge air cooler
  1. The fender apron is serviced as indicated.
  2. Before commencing this procedure make sure that you are aware of all Health and Safety requirements.For additional information, refer to: Health and Safety Precautions (100-00 General Information, Description and Operation).
  3. For further information on the methods, tools and fixings used in this procedure refer to the body repairs - general information section.
    4. NOTES:

    • Where breakstem fasteners (BSF) removal is instructed remove fastener shaft with a 4mm punch and remove the head with a 6.5mm drill bit. Clean all the drilled holes to make sure they are free from debris.

    • Where self piercing rivets (SPR's) removal is instructed use either the approved SPR tool or 5.3mm drill bit.

    • Any joints sealed as part of vehicle manufacture must be sealed as part of the repair.

  4. For additional information, refer to: Front Bumper (501-19 Bumpers, Removal and Installation).
  5. For additional information, refer to: Front Fender (501-27 Front End Sheet Metal Repairs, Removal and Installation).
  6. For additional information, refer to: Fender Splash Shield (501-02 Front End Body Panels, Removal and Installation).
  7. For additional information, refer to: Hood (501-02 Front End Body Panels, Removal and Installation).
  8. For additional information, refer to: Left Pedestrian Protection Hood Actuator (501-20C Pedestrian Protection System, Removal and Installation) / Right Pedestrian Protection Hood Actuator (501-20C Pedestrian Protection System, Removal and Installation).
  9. For additional information, refer to: Hood Latch Panel (501-02 Front End Body Panels, Removal and Installation).
  10. NOTE:

    Right hand only.

    For additional information, refer to: Anti-Lock Brake System Control Module (206-09 Anti-Lock Control - Stability Assist, Removal and Installation).
  11. NOTE:

    Right hand only.

    For additional information, refer to: Windshield Washer Reservoir (501-16 Wipers and Washers, Removal and Installation).
  12. NOTE:

    Left hand only.

    For additional information, refer to: Air Cleaner (303-12A Intake Air Distribution and Filtering - INGENIUM I4 2.0L Diesel, Removal and Installation) / Air Cleaner (303-12B Intake Air Distribution and Filtering - GTDi 2.0L Petrol, Removal and Installation) / Left Air Cleaner (303-12C Intake Air Distribution and Filtering - V6 S/C 3.0L Petrol, Removal and Installation).
  13. NOTE:

    Right hand only.

    For additional information, refer to: Right Air Cleaner (303-12C Intake Air Distribution and Filtering - V6 S/C 3.0L Petrol, Removal and Installation).
  14. NOTE:

    Left hand only.

    For additional information, refer to: Coolant Expansion Tank (303-03C Engine Cooling - V6 S/C 3.0L Petrol, Removal and Installation) / Coolant Expansion Tank (303-03C Engine Cooling - V6 S/C 3.0L Petrol, Removal and Installation) / Coolant Expansion Tank (303-03C Engine Cooling - V6 S/C 3.0L Petrol, Removal and Installation).
  15. NOTE:

    Left hand only.

    For additional information, refer to: Charge Air Cooler (303-12C Intake Air Distribution and Filtering - V6 S/C 3.0L Petrol, Removal and Installation).
  16. Release and position the wiring harness and the fuse boxes to one side.
  17. NOTE:

    Left hand only.

    Release and position the brake pipes to one side.
  18. NOTE:

    Right hand only.

    Release and position the air conditioning (A/C) pipes to one side.
  19. Remove the SPR's as indicated.
  20. Carefully build an expansion space between the A-pillar outer panel and the fender apron panel as indicated.
  21. Carefully separate the joints and remove the fender apron panel.
Installation
CAUTION:

Where structural adhesive is to be applied between surfaces it is essential compression is applied to the joints during the curing period.

NOTES:

  • BSF installation involves using the Land Rover approved BSF installation tool and requires a hole diameter of 6.5mm.

  • SPR installation requires using the Land Rover approved SPR tool.

  1. Remove rivet remnants.
  2. Dress the flanges where necessary.
  3. Offer up the new fender apron panel and clamp into position. Check alignment if correct proceed to next step, if not rectify and recheck before proceeding.
  4. Drill holes where the BSF's are to be installed as indicated.
  5. Remove the new fender apron panel.
  6. Deburr the drilled holes.
  7. Using a fine bristle disc, clean and prepare the panel surfaces.
  8. Apply the coupling agent where the Jaguar recommended bonding material is to be applied and allow to dry.
  9. NOTE:

    Make sure a continuous bead of adhesive surrounds fixing holes.

    Apply a 5mm zig zag bead of the Jaguar recommended bonding material as indicated.
  10. Offer up the new fender apron panel and clamp into position. Check alignment if correct proceed to next step, if not rectify and recheck before proceeding.
  11. Install the BSF's and the SPR's as indicated.
  12. Remove any excess adhesive.
  13. Make sure that any open or exposed panel joints are correctly sealed.
  14. Make sure corrosion protection is applied to all areas affected by repair.
  15. The installation of associated panels and components is the reversal of removal procedure.
