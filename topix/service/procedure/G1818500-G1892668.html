Removal
CAUTIONS:

  • If the exterior door handle is received pre-built, or alternatively, once the exterior door handle has been assembled, under no circumstances should the exterior door handle be disassembled as this could cause damage to the vehicle.

  • Protect the surrounding paintwork to avoid damage.

NOTES:

  • This procedure contains illustrations showing certain components removed to provide extra clarity.

  • This procedure contains some variation in the illustrations depending on the vehicle specification, but the essential information is always correct.


  1. CAUTIONS:

    • This step is only required if the exterior door handle is received as kit of parts.

    • Under no circumstances should the exterior door handle be disassembled as this could cause damage to the vehicle.

    If required, assemble the exterior door handle.
    Refer to: Exterior Door Handle Assembly (501-14 Handles, Locks, Latches and Entry Systems, General Procedures).
  2. Disconnect the startup battery ground cable.
    Refer to: Startup Battery Disconnect and Connect (414-01 Battery, Mounting and Cables, General Procedures).
  3. To avoid accidental deployment, wait at least 2 minutes after disconnect the startup battery ground cable.
  4. Remove the front door trim panel.
    Refer to: Front Door Trim Panel (501-05 Interior Trim and Ornamentation, Removal and Installation).
    • Disconnect the electrical connector.
    • Release the 4 wiring harness clips.
    • Remove the 8 bolts.
    • Remove the closing panel.
  7. Disconnect the electrical connector.
  8. NOTE:

    Driver side only.

    Remove the access cover.
  9. NOTE:

    Loosen the bolt, but do not fully remove.

    • Remove the access cover.
    • Loosen the bolt.
  10. NOTE:

    Driver side only.

    Remove the door lock cylinder.
  11. NOTE:

    Passenger side only.

    Remove the cover.
  12. Remove the exterior front door handle.
Installation
  1. Install the exterior front door handle.
  2. NOTE:

    Driver side only.

    Install the door lock cylinder.
    • Tighten the bolt.
      Torque: 4.1 Nm
    • Install the access cover.
  4. NOTE:

    Driver side only.

    Install the access cover.
  5. Connect the electrical connector to the exterior front door handle.
    • Install the closing panel.
    • Install and tighten the 8 bolts.
      Torque: 5 Nm
    • Install the 4 wiring harness clips.
    • Connect the electrical connectors.
  8. Install the front door trim panel.
    Refer to: Front Door Trim Panel (501-05 Interior Trim and Ornamentation, Removal and Installation).
  9. Connect the startup battery ground cable.
    Refer to: Startup Battery Disconnect and Connect (414-01 Battery, Mounting and Cables, General Procedures).
