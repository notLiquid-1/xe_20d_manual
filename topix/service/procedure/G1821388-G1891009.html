Vehicle Identification Number (VIN)

The official vehicle identification number (VIN) for title and registration purposes is stamped on a metal plate and fastened to the instrument panel. It is positioned close on the left-hand side of the vehicle and is visible from the outside.

The VIN is also located on the vehicle certification label.

Engine Number(s)

Engine Number - 2.0L Diesel

The serial number is stamped/etched on the left side of the cylinder block behind the fuel pump.

Engine Number - 2.0L Petrol

The engine serial number is etched into the right side of the engine cylinder block.

Engine Number - 3.0L Supercharged

The serial number is stamped on an engine web on the left side of the cylinder block behind the engine mounting.

Automatic Transmission Serial Number

The transmission serial number is on a plate attached to the left side of the transmission casing.

Manual Transmission Serial Number

The manual transmission serial number is displayed on a metal label or bar code attached to the left side of the transmission casing.
