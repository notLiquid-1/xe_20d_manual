Rear End Sheet Metal Repairs

Back Panel Inner (G1794526)

REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION

Loading...

Removal
NOTE:

In this procedure the back panel inner is installed in conjunction with:

  • Rear bumper
  • Rear armature
  • Rear lamp assembly
  • Loadspace trim panel
  • Auxiliary junction box (AJB)
  • Subwoofer amplifier
  • Loadspace scuff plate trim panel
  • Back panel outer
  1. The back panel inner is serviced as indicated.
  2. Before commencing this procedure make sure that you are aware of all Health and Safety requirements. For additional information, refer to: Health and Safety Precautions (100-00 General Information, Description and Operation).
  3. For further information on the methods, tools and fixings used in this procedure refer to the body repairs - general information section.
    4. NOTES:

    • Any joints sealed as part of vehicle manufacture must be sealed as part of the repair.

    • Make sure a zinc rich primer is applied to any bare metal surfaces.

  4. For additional information, refer to: Rear Bumper (501-19 Bumpers, Removal and Installation).
  5. For additional information, refer to: Rear Armature (501-19 Bumpers, Removal and Installation).
  6. For additional information, refer to: Rear Lamp Assembly (417-01 Exterior Lighting, Removal and Installation).
  7. For additional information, refer to: Loadspace Trim Panel (501-05 Interior Trim and Ornamentation, Removal and Installation) / Left Loadspace Trim Panel (501-05 Interior Trim and Ornamentation, Removal and Installation) / Right Loadspace Trim Panel (501-05 Interior Trim and Ornamentation, Removal and Installation).
  8. For additional information, refer to: Rear Junction Box (418-00 Module Communications Network, Removal and Installation).
  9. For additional information, refer to: Subwoofer Amplifier (415-01 Information and Entertainment System, Removal and Installation).
  10. For additional information, refer to: Front Scuff Plate Trim Panel (501-05 Interior Trim and Ornamentation, Removal and Installation).
  11. For additional information, refer to: Back Panel Outer (501-30 Rear End Sheet Metal Repairs, Removal and Installation).
  12. Release and position the spare wheel well wiring harness to one side.
  13. Remove the spot welds as indicated.
  14. Remove the spot welds as indicated.
  15. Carefully separate the joints and remove the back panel inner.
Installation
  1. Dress the flanges where necessary.
  2. Offer up the back panel inner and clamp into position. Check alignment if correct proceed to next step, if not rectify and recheck before proceeding.
  3. Drill holes where the metal inert gas (MIG) plug welds are to be installed as indicated.
  4. Remove the back panel inner.
  5. Deburr the drilled holes.
  6. Clean and prepare the panel surfaces.
  7. Offer up the back panel inner and clamp into position. Check alignment if correct proceed to next step, if not rectify and recheck before proceeding.
  8. Make sure that the reinforcement brackets are correctly located before installing the back panel inner.
  9. Install the MIG plug welds as indicated.
  10. Install the spot welds as indicated.
  11. Dress the welds.
  12. Make sure that any open or exposed panel joints are correctly sealed.
  13. Make sure corrosion protection is applied to all areas affected by repair.
  14. The installation of associated panels and components is the reversal of removal procedure.
