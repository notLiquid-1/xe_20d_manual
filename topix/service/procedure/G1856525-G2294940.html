Removal
NOTES:

  • This procedure contains some variation in the illustration depending on the vehicle specification, but essential information is always correct.

  • This procedure contains illustrations showing certain components removed to provide extra clarity.


  1. Raise and support the vehicle on a suitable 2 post lift.
    Refer to: Lifting (100-02 Jacking and Lifting, Description and Operation).
    General Equipment: 2 post lift
  2. Remove the rear differential case.
    Refer to: Differential vehicles with heavy duty differential (205-02, Removal and Installation).
    Refer to: Differential vehicles without heavy duty differential (205-02, Removal and Installation).
  3. Remove the bush.
    Special Tool(s): 204-335
Installation
  1. NOTE:

    Make sure the new bushes are installed in the correct orientation.

    Install the new bush.
    Special Tool(s): 204-275, 204-601, 303-1121, 204-274
  2. Install the rear differential case.
    Refer to: Differential vehicles with heavy duty differential (205-02, Removal and Installation).
    Refer to: Differential vehicles without heavy duty differential (205-02, Removal and Installation).
