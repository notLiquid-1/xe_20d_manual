Fluid leaks

After the road test, the vehicle must be checked for fluid leaks.

Check:

  • The engine bay for any fluid leaks and that all pipes and hoses are securely clipped in their anchorages.
  • That no chafing has occurred or is likely to occur in the future.

Malfunction Indicator Lamp illumination

If any fault lamps are illuminated, connect the Jaguar Land Rover-approved battery power supply and diagnostic equipment to the vehicle and rectify any problems found.

Smart keys

Make sure both Smart keys operate correctly and fit the correct type approval labels (where applicable).

Tire pressures

Set the tire pressures to the recommended value on the B-pillar and confirm it matches within the instrument cluster.

NOTE:

the ignition must be switched ON and the engine switched OFF.

Using the steering wheel controls, select Menu > Vehicle Info > Tire Pressures > Handbook Pressures.

Confirm the displayed tire pressures match the actual tire pressures.

Airbag warning label - National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA) - mandatory fitment of Airbag Warning label.

The airbag warning label MUST be fitted to the vehicle and viewable by the customer upon vehicle delivery.

Airbag Warning label

Fit the airbag warning label:

  • Open the glove compartment to gain access to mount the airbag warning label.
  • Loop the elastic around the single latch.
  • Close the lid and leave the label hanging on the outside of the glove compartment so that the label is clearly visible to the owner of the new vehicle at the time of handover.

Passport to Service booklet

Make sure vehicle details are included in the Passport to Service booklet; endorse with retailer stamp.

Vehicle literature; floor mats

Place literature pack and mats in the vehicle.

PDI form

Retain copy of the PDI form in the Customer/Vehicle History File.

Valet the vehicle

Make sure the vehicle is clean prior to customer delivery.

NOTE:

Vehicles being returned to storage: set all tire pressures with the tires cold to 3.6 bar (52psi) and the vehicle must be placed into transportation mode, after which a full PDI must be repeated before customer handover. Refer to the New Vehicle Storage Manual (see TOPIx, Maintenance, Storage & PDI) for full details.
